Privacy Policy

MousePaw Media believes strongly in the respect of visitors' and users' privacy. We have adopted this privacy policy to guide how MousePaw Media gathers, stores, and uses information from the use of MousePaw Media products and online sites by users.

This policy is current as of its last revision date. However, please note that this policy may be amended from time to time to reflect changes and additions to the privacy policy. Please check back for the most recent version before relying on any of the provisions in this privacy policy.

Collection and Use of Information

By using MousePaw Media's online sites and products, users agree that MousePaw Media may collect and aggregate information, individual information, and personally identifiable information, as defined below. MousePaw Media may share aggregate information and individual information with other parties. MousePaw Media shall not share personally identifiable information with other parties, except as described in the policy below.

"Aggregate information" is information that describes the habits, usage patterns, and demographics of users as a group but does not describe or reveal the identity of any particular user.

"Individual information" is information about a user that is presented in a form distinguishable from information relating to other users but not in a form that personally identifies any user or enables the recipient to communicate directly with any user unless agreed to by the user in advance of such communication. This information may be used to improve MousePaw Media's products and online sites, for internal marketing studies, or simply to collect demographic information about MousePaw Media's users.

MousePaw Media may use customer contact information provided by users to send information about MousePaw Media, including news about product updates, contests, events, and other promotional materials, but only if the users agree to receive such communications. Except in the cases describes below, MousePaw Media will not share personally identifiable information with any third party unless the user agrees to such disclosure in advance. "Personally identifiable information" consists of a user's name, email address, physical address, or other data about the user that enables the recipient to personally identify the user. While MousePaw Media collects personally identifiable information on a voluntary basis, for certain products and online sites, MousePaw Media's collection of personally identifiable information may be a requirement for access to the product or site. Personally identifiable information will be processed and stored by MousePaw Media in databases situated in the United States. MousePaw Media may allow third parties performing services under contract with MousePaw Media to access stored information but such access shall only be to the extent necessary to provide these services.

Personally identifiable information protected under this privacy policy and collected from users may be done in conjunction with associates under agreement with MousePaw Media. If an associate of MousePaw Media is collecting such personally identifiable information within one of our products of online sites, MousePaw Media will make users aware of this at the time the information is gathered.

Furthermore, external websites and companies with links to and from MousePaw Media's online sites and products may collect personal information about users. MousePaw Media's privacy policy does not extend to these external websites and companies. Please refer directly to these companies and websites regarding their privacy policies.

Children Under the Age of 13

In compliance with the Child Online Privacy Protection Act, MousePaw Media will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from any person age 12 and under. MousePaw Media encourages parents to instruct their children to never give out personal information when online. Parents who are concerned about the transfer of personal information from their children may contact us at privacy@nullmousepawmedia.com to obtain a record of any information held by MousePaw Media and have it removed at their request.

Communities, etc.

MousePaw Media may, at times, provide means of online communication between users, including but not limited to forums, chatrooms, and bulletin boards. Any information that is disclosed in these online communities is not necessarily regulated by MousePaw Media or covered under this privacy policy. Users should exercise caution when deciding to disclose personal information in such online communities.

Opt-In/Out-Out

MousePaw Media gives users the option of discontinuing receipt of promotional email communications from MousePaw Media. Instructions for discontinuing receipt of promotional email communications from MousePaw Media will be provided in such subsequent email communications to users.

Storage and Security of Information

Personally identifiable information will be processed and stored by MousePaw Media in databases hosted in the United States. MousePaw Media has taken reasonable steps to protect the information users share with us, as has our web hosting service.

Corrections, Updates and Removal of Information

If you are concerned about the accuracy of personal information maintained by MousePaw Media or would like your personal information removed from MousePaw Media's records, please contact us by emailing privacy@nullmousepawmedia.com for review, update, or removal of personal information.

Contacting MousePaw Media

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, or the privacy practices of MousePaw Media, please contact us by e-mailing privacy@nullmousepawmedia.com.
