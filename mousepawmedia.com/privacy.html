Privacy Policy

MousePaw Media believes strongly in the respect of visitors' and users' privacy. We have adopted this privacy policy to guide how MousePaw Media gathers, stores, and uses information from the use of MousePaw Media products and online sites by users.

This policy is current as of its last revision date. However, please note that this policy may be amended from time to time to reflect changes and additions to the privacy policy. Please check back for the most recent version before relying on any of the provisions in this privacy policy.

Collection and Use of Information

We allow you to create accounts on some of our websites, either directly or through a GitHub OAuth account integration. Account creation and use may require some personal data. We store the following data as provided by you,

  • Your email address,
  • Your legal name,

We value your privacy, and will not disclose your personally identifiable information, except as required by law, with any third parties, unless you give us permission first. In most cases, this information is stored indefinitely.

We do assume that if you contribute any work to our project, you'd like credit. If you don't want your username associated with your contributions, please let us know at privacy@nullmousepawmedia.com.

If you choose to publicly share information using our platform, including via your user profile, you are giving all other users the right to view that information. Our Privacy Policy doesn't cover that, and we really can't do anything about it. Please use caution!

Personally identifiable information will be processed and stored by MousePaw Media in MySQL databases hosted in the United States by MousePaw Media and by Linode. We've taken reasonable steps to protect the information users share with us, as has our web hosting service.

External websites and companies with links to and from our online sites and products may collect personal information about users. Our privacy policy does not extend to these external websites and companies. Please refer directly to these companies and websites regarding their privacy policies.

Children Under the Age of 13

In compliance with the Child Online Privacy Protection Act, MousePaw Media will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from any person age 12 and under. MousePaw Media encourages parents to instruct their children to never give out personal information when online. Parents who are concerned about the transfer of personal information from their children may contact us at privacy@nullmousepawmedia.com to obtain a record of any information held by MousePaw Media and have it removed at their request.

Corrections, Updates and Removal of Information

If you are concerned about the accuracy of personal information maintained by MousePaw Media or would like your personal information removed from our records, please contact us by emailing privacy@nullmousepawmedia.com for review, update, or removal of personal information.

Contacting MousePaw Media

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, or the privacy practices of MousePaw Media, please contact us by e-mailing privacy@nullmousepawmedia.com.
