MousePaw ID

Your MousePaw ID is your key to all MousePaw Media development and collaboration tools. It provides access to Phabricator, Nextcloud, and Jenkins.

MousePaw IDs are issued for staff, interns (including community interns), and high-impact volunteers.

If you have problems with or questions about your MousePaw ID, please contact us eco@nullmousepawmedia.com

FAQs

Do I need a MousePaw ID for Phabricator?

If you just want to access Phabricator as a volunteer, you can log in with a GitHub account instead.

Do I qualify for a MousePaw ID?

All interns, including community interns, are automatically issued MousePaw IDs. Internship graduates ordinarily retain their MousePaw IDs.

If you regularly make quality contributions to MousePaw Media projects as a volunteer, and you need access to our CI pipeline and/or our planning tools, you can request a MousePaw ID by contacting developers@nullmousepawmedia.com

Can I change my MousePaw ID password?

Because of the way the MousePaw ID system is built, it is not currently possible (or practical) for you to set your own password. You'll be issued a randomly generated passphrase when your MousePaw ID is created. If you'd like a new passphrase, just contact us. (We take steps to verify it's you.)

I believe my MousePaw ID is compromised.

If at any time you suspect that someone has your MousePaw ID credentials, please contact us right away! We can take immediate steps to change your password or lock down your MousePaw ID.

Someone at MousePaw Media asked for my ID. Do I give it?

No! Our IT department has processes in place if it becomes necessary to access accounts linked to your MousePaw ID. We will never ask for your MousePaw ID under any circumstances. If you have been asked to provide this information, it's a phishing scheme, and should be reported to us immediately.
